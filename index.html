About Me

Hi, my name is Derek and this is my portfolio, here you'll get a glimpse of who I am and what I can do. I'm a kid that lived most of my life in a small town known as Dodge City. When I'm not busy with school I'm either spending my free time drawing, writing, working on personal video game related projects, or relaxing by watching Ultraman. I hope we can get along and move forward to make something great.
